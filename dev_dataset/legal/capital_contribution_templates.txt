IMPORTANT NOTES:

1. CONTEMPORANEOUS DOCUMENTATION: Complete these forms at the time of each
   transaction, not retroactively. Courts give more weight to contemporaneous
   documentation.

2. CONSISTENCY: Use these forms every quarter, even if amounts are small.
   Consistency demonstrates respect for corporate formalities.

3. SOLVENCY TESTS: ALWAYS perform solvency tests before distributions.
   Distributing when insolvent can pierce the LLC veil and expose you to
   personal liability.

4. SEPARATION: Keep LLC funds completely separate from personal funds. Never
   commingle.

5. BUSINESS PURPOSE: Only use LLC funds for legitimate business purposes
   documented in the Operating Agreement.

6. RETAIN RECORDS: Keep all LLC records for minimum 7 years (IRS requirement)
   or longer for legal protection.

7. OPERATING AGREEMENT: These templates assume you have a written Operating
   Agreement. If not, create one immediately.

8. PROFESSIONAL ADVICE: These templates provide documentation but are not a
   substitute for legal or tax advice. Consult professionals for complex
   situations.
