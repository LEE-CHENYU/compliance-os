  CRITICAL NOTE FOR YOUR CASE:
    As an F-1 nonresident alien (exempt from SPT for first 5 calendar years),
    you are NOT a "United States person" under IRC Section 7701(a)(30).
    Therefore, for tax years 2023, 2024, and 2025, you should NOT need:
      - FBAR (only applies to US persons)
      - Form 8938 (only applies to specified persons — generally US persons)
      - Form 3520 (only applies to US persons receiving foreign gifts)

    The FBAR, 8938, and 3520 obligations will begin in the FIRST tax year
    you become a resident alien (likely 2028 or later, depending on visa
    status changes). If any CPA firm tells you to file these now, that is
    a red flag indicating they may not understand the NRA distinction.

    The forms you DO need:
      - Form 1040-NR (corrected, for 2023, 2024, and 2025)
      - Form 8843 (each year)
      - Form 5472 + pro forma 1120 (for the SMLLC — this IS required
        regardless of NRA status, as it's an entity-level filing)
      - Schedule C on Form 1040-NR (for ECI from the SMLLC)
