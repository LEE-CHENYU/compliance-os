H-1B SELF-PETITION / BUSINESS OWNER CONTEXT (general):
  As of January 17, 2025, USCIS H-1B modernization rule allows entrepreneurs
  to self-petition for H-1B even if sole owner of company, IF they can establish
  valid employer-employee relationship (right to hire, pay, fire, supervise, or
  control work).

  Requirements:
    - LLC, S-corp, C-corp, or other corporate entity (NOT sole proprietorship)
    - Federal EIN obtained
    - Must show separation between owner and employee roles
    - Must demonstrate control/supervision structure
