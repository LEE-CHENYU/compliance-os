─────────────────────────────────
Q1: Advisable to start CPT at Pacific River Capital to establish employer-employee
    relationship before H-1B petition?

    CPT rules — check with school first (see below).

    both entities (BSGC and Yangtze)?

    If school does not allow it, then do NOT pursue CPT at Yangtze.

PASSPORT RENEWAL (G88855571, expires June 30, 2026)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q1: Do you still need the current passport for H-1B registration or
    upcoming filings?
A:  Registration is good with current passport. Valid passport required
    for H-1B full petition (filing window: April–June 2026). If renewal
    is complete by then, submit both old and new passports.

Q2: Good window to send for renewal, or wait until after a milestone?
A:  Sooner rather than later — foreign nationals are required to keep a
    valid passport at all times.
