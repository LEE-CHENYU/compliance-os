# Founder Execution Timeline

As of 2026-03-26.

Purpose: capture founder-side operating constraints that now matter for both product execution and immigration/compliance timing.

## Operating Sequence

1. Bring Compliance OS online.
2. Get a first set of real users to test both the product idea and the team.
3. Use that initial signal for investor outreach, rather than leading with fundraising before there is any user proof.

## Pre-Petition Requirements

Before the H-1B full petition stage, the company should look operational rather than hypothetical:

- Compliance OS is online and usable.
- At least two employees are onboarded.
- Basic employer evidence is coherent: product is live, team exists, operating story is concrete.
- Passport-renewal reservation is already booked with enough buffer before petition work.

## Timeboxes

### Now through 2026-04-15

- Finalize all tax-related work and supporting text/doc package by the April 15 tax deadline.
- Keep tax, entity, and immigration facts aligned so nothing that goes into the petition package contradicts the filing record.

### Before the H-1B petition-prep window hardens

- Make the passport-renewal reservation before the current leave period ends.
- Bring Compliance OS online.
- Onboard two employees.
- Make sure the public/company-facing surface is live before petition preparation.

### Before 2026-10-01

Reach at least one of these states before the October H-1B activation/start-date milestone:

- fund raised, or
- initial cash flow established, or
- founder placed on payroll.

The point is not abstract momentum. The point is operational viability before the October immigration/payroll transition.

## Decision Rule

- User proof before investor outreach.
- Operational reality before petition narrative.
- Cash flow or financing before October.
